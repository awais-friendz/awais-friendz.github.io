Hello My Friends !

ASSALAM-O-ALIKUM! Meray yaaro School life bohat acha time tha kisi cheese ki tension nahi thi. School is tarha tha kay ghar ho. School kay karnamy souch kar hassi ati hay. Ab chah kar bhi wo din wapis to nahi a saktay magar un ki yaaday hamysha hamary sath rahay gi. School kay dino ko sab say acha banay wali cheese hay DOST. Aj may un DOSTO aur us school kay liay jaha itna acha time guzra kuch kahna chahta hun.

Kia din thay wo

jab dost ka na ana

sab say bada dukh tha

ab to koi apana ho wohi kafi hay

Umar thi wo jab homework or test

duniya ka sab say bada stress tha

aur teacher ki dant sab say badi insult

Duniya kia thi

yay to jana hi nahi tha

khushsio ka daman tha

na dar na khauf

Mazaq aur lateefo ki qeemat

na jani thi tab

ab to souch kar hi yaday taza hoti hay

Bachpan kay jhagray thy jin say

aj un ko bhi milnay ko dil tarasta hay

kia din thay wo

Jab teacher ka na ana party jaisa tha

par exam kay waqat

bohat dar lagta tha

Jhagry bohat hotay thay

kuch khtay kuch meethay

kuch dyr kay liay naraz

magar jald hi dobara dost bann jatay thay

School jatay waqat bhi dukhi aur shortay waqat bhi

kis nay jana tha jo safar ansuo say shuro huwa tha

wo ansuo par hi khatam hoga

Kia din thay wo

jab dost ko detay thay dilasa

yari say badhta tha hosla

yaar ki qeemat to tab pata chali

jab bichadnay ka gam dil may bas gaya

discipline ko todna bhi ek masti thi

aisa agar na kiya hota to school life hi bekar thi

kia din thay wo

jab esharo may hi samjhay jati thi bateein

aj to lakh kahnay par bhi nahi samajhtay duniya walay

logon ki galtiyo ko accept karna sikaha tha

ab to sara zaman galtiya dhondnay par laga hay

agar ab bhi zindgi kay us modr par ho yaro

ji lo apni student life ko

yay pal na kabhi aye gay

ek bar jo chalay gay to gam kai saary day jaye gay

Rotay rotay jo hansa dey us ka naam dosti hay

tension may bhi jo masti kara dey us ka naam dosti hay

school ki yado ko yadgar bana dey

jab apany hi door ho jai koi pass nazar na aye

uss wzat jo sath nibhay uss rishtay ka naam dosti hay

hazar bahany bana lo chahay

jo phir bhi baat mana lay us ka naam dosti hay

galtia karnay par jo dant may sharing karey uss dost ka andaz dosti hay

pahlay plans banay phir plans cancel kry aur dannt khay

phir bhi maan may koi gila na rakhay wo pyar dosti hay

apas may jitna marzi larr lo magar

kisi aur ki zuban say apnay dost kay liay kuch sun kar jo gusa aye

wo ehsas dosti hay

jab lagat tha kay college school yay waqat tham jaye

na farwell aye na ham kabhi door jaye

wo door jane ka dar dosti hay

kuch tasveero say kuch qisso say aur un qisso say ankho may ansu ajay
